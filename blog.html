Objective/ Profile summary

The objective is the first box that the employer will read in your CV/Resume. The objective must be good. You may not have work experience yet, butyou may be the best candidate among the others willing applicante for the job.

It is worthwhile to spend some time on writing 3-5 lines about yourself, in order to interest the employer. In this candidacy at the vert beginning. The objective should be short & specific. Describe your strengths, skills, interests & place information about the branch you are looking for employment in.
